2. Jump Rope

Jump rope for 5 minutes to improve footwork and endurance. This is great for overall conditioning and coordination.

3. Heavy Bag Work

Work on combinations and power with the heavy bag. Start with 3 rounds of 2 minutes each, focusing on throwing jab-cross combos.

Boxing Tips for Beginners

  • Focus on Form: Always make sure your form is correct. This will help prevent injuries and improve your effectiveness in the ring.
  • Conditioning is Key: Boxing is not just about power, but endurance. Make sure to work on your stamina with cardio and strength training.
  • Footwork: Great footwork is essential. Practice moving around the ring and maintaining balance at all times.
  • Stay Consistent: Progress comes with regular practice. Make boxing part of your routine to build skills and improve over time.
